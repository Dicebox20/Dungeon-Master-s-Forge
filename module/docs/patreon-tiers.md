# Dungeon Master's Forge Patreon Tier Structure

## Core Philosophy

Dungeon Master's Forge does not limit imagination.

Every tier can create dramatic, legendary, strange, funny, overpowered, or deeply flavorful magic items. Users can name items however they want, choose any rarity label, write any theme, and make intentionally wild items for their own tables.

The tiers limit mechanical complexity, automation, monthly volume, and advanced Foundry integration, not creativity.

## Apprentice

- Price: Free
- Suggested volume: 30 items per month
- Focus: basic Foundry-ready DMG-style items

Includes:

- item name, type, rarity, attunement, description, flavor, activation, targets, saves, damage, uses, recharge
- preview before import
- weapons, armor, equipment, consumables, and wondrous items
- flat bonuses to AC, saves, attacks, damage, and checks
- simple `+1`, `+2`, `+3` style bonuses
- bonus damage dice
- simple resistances
- up to 2 SRD spells

Limits:

- no custom conditional effects
- no advanced Active Effects
- no Midi-QOL or Activity Macro automation
- no summoning automation
- no realm-transfer effects
- no item-linked scene packages

## Adept

- Price: $4.99 per month
- Suggested volume: 150 to 200 items per month
- Focus: custom mechanical effects

Adds:

- custom passive effects
- conditional bonuses
- basic Active Effects
- more unusual damage and effect combinations
- more flexible charge and recharge behavior
- up to 4 SRD spells
- clean Foundry-supported advantage and disadvantage style effects

Limits:

- no complex macros
- no Midi-QOL automation
- no token movement automation
- no generated realm scenes
- no full summoning automation

## Journeyman

- Price: $9.99 per month
- Suggested volume: 500 to 750 items per month
- Focus: advanced item building without full automation

Adds:

- up to 7 SRD spells
- advanced Active Effects
- scaling damage
- shared charge pools
- multi-activity items
- more complex save, attack, target, and area setups
- better support for staffs, grimoires, relics, cursed items, and multi-use magic items

Limits:

- no full Midi-QOL macro generation
- no Activity Macro generation
- no automated summoning packages
- no realm-transfer scene packages
- no Monk's Active Tile Triggers integration

## Master

- Price: $19.99 per month
- Suggested volume: 1,500 to 2,000 items per month
- Focus: automation, macros, and advanced Foundry behavior

Adds:

- 10 or more SRD spells
- Midi-QOL support
- Activity Macro support
- advanced item automation
- summoning items
- automated chat messages
- safe token and actor manipulation
- complex status effects
- multi-step item abilities

Limits:

- no Realmforged scene packages
- no premade item-linked map or scene integration
- no Monk's Active Tile Triggers scene automation
- no Founding Patron credits

## Founding Patron

- Price: $49.99 per month
- Focus: premium early-supporter tier with advanced item-linked scene features

Adds:

- highest monthly generation allowance
- early access to experimental features
- priority feedback consideration
- project credits as a Founding Patron
- Realmforged Items
- Spellforged Realm Variants
- optional bespoke item-icon image generation for generated items
- optional Monk's Active Tile Triggers integration for supported item-linked scenes

## Realmforged Items

Realmforged Items are special item packages that include:

1. the item itself
2. a premade integrated scene template
3. a macro that sends the target token to that scene
4. optional return logic
5. optional Monk's Active Tile Triggers support

Dungeon Master's Forge is not a battle map generator. Realmforged scenes are premade, item-linked templates selected and configured as part of an item's mechanics.

## Spellforged Realm Variants

Good candidates for Founding Patron realm-linked spell variants include:

- Banishment variants
- Maze variants
- Imprisonment-style effects
- offensive Plane Shift style effects
- dream prison effects
- pocket dimension spells

The generator should clone the original spell and create a new variant rather than overwrite system content.
